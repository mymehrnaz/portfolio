Mehrnaz

Gender:feminine

Usage:Persian,Persian mythology

Meaning and history:

from persian مهر (mehr) meaning "sun" or
"friendship" and ناز (naz)
meaning "delight, comfort, glory of the sun".
This is the name of a character in the 10th-
cectury Persian epic the Shahnameh.
it is a relatively short name with 7 letters, and it
holds significant apeal due to it's powerful
meaning.

Related Names:

roots:mehr+naz(مهرناز)
